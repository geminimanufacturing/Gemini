About Gemini Manufacturing & Co.

Gemini Manufacturing & Co. is a specialised Fiber Reinforced Plastic (FRP) manufacturing company based in India, delivering durable, lightweight and design-driven FRP products for commercial, retail and architectural use.

Who We Are

At Gemini Manufacturing & Co., we combine material expertise with thoughtful design to create FRP products that stand the test of time. Our manufacturing approach focuses on precision moulding, surface finish quality and project-specific customisation.

We serve architects, retailers, visual merchandisers, landscapers and project developers who value reliability, aesthetics and long-term performance.

Our Product Divisions

Gemini Manufacturing & Co. operates through focused product verticals to ensure specialised expertise in every category.

Gemini Mannequins

Premium FRP mannequins for retail, fashion displays and exhibitions.

FRP Planters & Pots

Indoor and outdoor FRP planters designed for modern architectural spaces.

Sculptures & Props

Custom FRP sculptures and props for branding, decor and public installations.

Fountains & Profiles

Architectural FRP elements including fountains and structural profiles.

Why FRP

Fiber Reinforced Plastic offers exceptional strength-to-weight ratio, weather resistance and design flexibility. This makes FRP an ideal material for both indoor and outdoor applications where durability and aesthetics matter.
