About Gemini Manufacturing & Co.

Gemini Manufacturing & Co. is a leading FRP product manufacturer in India. Our sub-company, Gemini Mannequins, specializes in premium FRP mannequins, while our parent division produces high-quality planters, sculptures, props, fountains, and architectural profiles for commercial and residential applications.
