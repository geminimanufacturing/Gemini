Who We Are

Gemini Manufacturing & Co. is the parent company of Gemini Mannequins, specializing in high-quality FRP products including mannequins, planters, sculptures, props, fountains and architectural profiles.

Our focus lies in precision manufacturing, refined aesthetics and long-lasting materials designed for retail, commercial, architectural and landscape applications.
